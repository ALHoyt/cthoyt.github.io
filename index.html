Writing

In my freshman summer reading at Northeastern, author Atul Gawande insisted at the importance of writing. I was soon introduced to In the Pipeline on my first co-op at GSK. It's given me valuable insight into many facets of pharma and medicinal chemistry as a young scientist.

As I became interested in informatics, I realized there were few community sources of news. I want to make a habit of writing in graduate school to share my adventures as an American in Europe and about the science I learn. Part I is my adventure as a teaching assistant on Northeastern's Chemistry, Drug Discovery, and Development Dialogue. Part II is about what I'm learning in graduate school, and Parts III and beyond will come soon.
